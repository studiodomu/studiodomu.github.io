    bio: Giulia C. Borges is an Italo-Brazilian director, actress, and producer based in Milan (IT). Her work moves between documentary and fiction, exploring horror, psychological thriller, identity, and memory. She graduated in Filmmaking from the Civica Scuola di Cinema Luchino Visconti (2019) with the documentary La nave Davidò (MyArt Film Festival, Docucity/MUDEC) and directed Padre (2022), a musical short distributed by Gorilla Distribution and premiered at Wegiel Film Festival. Her upcoming horror short Teacher Andy (post-production) won Best Pitch at Figari Film Fest 2022, receiving post-production funding and becoming a WeShort Original, also part of ShortTS and ShortTO – Road to Euro Connection at Torino Film Festival and Clermont-Ferrand. Alongside directing, she works in film production on shorts and features selected at national and international festivals. She is currently developing dark-genre projects between Italy and Brazil.
